# Blog Content Guidelines

- Audience: prospective students, current learners, alumni, partners.
- Tone: inspiring, informative, community-focused.
- Structure: title, published date, author, category, hero image, 800-1200 words body, call-to-action.
- Visuals: high-resolution images, landscape orientation preferred, 16:9 aspect.
- Submission: email submissions to communications@csamzaccaria.rw with subject "Blog Submission".
